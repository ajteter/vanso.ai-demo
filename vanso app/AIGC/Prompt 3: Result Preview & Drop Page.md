Design a "Result Preview & Checkout" mobile screen for an AI music app. 
Style: Premium Dark Mode ("Sonic Aura" aesthetic), background , high-contrast white text, neon accents, and clean glassmorphism.

**Layout Structure:**

1. **Top Handle (Draft Logic):**
   - At the absolute top center, place a subtle horizontal pill/line (a drag handle). This implies the user can swipe the page down to close it (saving to drafts).

2. **Top Section (The Audio Player - Top 1/3 of screen):**
   - A large, visually stunning square Cover Art taking up the top third. The image should be an abstract, fluid AI-generated gradient art.
   - On the top-right corner of the cover art, place a subtle "Camera" or "Refresh" icon indicating the cover can be changed.
   - **Inline Player Controls:** Directly overlaying or right below the cover art: A minimalist, sleek Play/Pause button (show a "Pause" icon to imply it's auto-playing) and a slender audio progress bar.

3. **Metadata Section (Scrollable Body):**
   - **Title (Editable):** Large, bold typography (e.g., "Neon Rain"). Add a subtle "Pencil ✏️" icon next to it or style it as a borderless input field to show it's editable.
   
   - **Language & Style (Read-only Card):**
     - A compact glassmorphism card/box. 
     - Top right of the card: A small "Copy" icon.
     - Content inside: "Language: English • Style: Synthwave, Lo-fi beat". The box should hint that it is scrollable if content overflows. Read-only text .
   
   - **Lyrics (Read-only Card):**
     - Another glassmorphism box below, max-height set so it scrolls. 
     - Top right of the card: A small "Copy" icon.
     - Content inside: 3-4 lines of placeholder lyrics with tags like "[Verse]" and "[Chorus]". Read-only text.

   - **Description (Editable Input):**
     - A clean text area box. 
     - Placeholder: "Add a vibe or story behind this track (Optional)..."

4. **Footer Action Bar (Sticky at Bottom):**
   - A floating, glassmorphism bar fixed at the bottom.
   - Flex row with two buttons:
   - **Left Button (30% width):** Secondary style (dark gray bg or outline). Icon + Text: "🔄 Reroll".
   - **Right Button (70% width):** Primary solid button with a vivid gradient background. Text: "🚀 Post to World" (Bold, white text).
